CASE STUDIES

Selected work

01 · META

Teaching Meta's production LLM to speak Thai

Meta's production LLM underperformed in Thai — a low-resource language where generic multilingual data falls short. I paired native-speaker judgment with rubric-driven eval loops and targeted curation: 2,000+ quality-scored examples feeding every fine-tune round.

"Curation beats volume when the evaluator speaks the language."

02 · MERCOR

Stress-testing models for three frontier labs

Three frontier research labs needed to know exactly where their models fail — before users did. I built cross-domain evaluation frameworks and adversarial edge-case suites across coding, chemistry, cybersecurity, data analysis, reasoning, and applied AI, feeding RLHF and SFT alignment pipelines.

"A model is only as good as the hardest question you ask it."

03 · SNORKEL AI

Building the gyms where coding agents train

Flaky environments poison training data. I designed multi-step coding-agent tasks that force genuine end-to-end reasoning, then treated the environments themselves as production software — debugged, hardened, verified with full test suites.

"Agent quality starts with environment quality."
